Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how LT ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use our e-learning website, LT (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care. The Service is provided free of charge and is supported by advertising.


1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information directly from you when you register for an account and use our Service. The types of personal data we collect include:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize your experience.
  • Email Address: For account creation, login, communication, and chat functionalities.
  • Grade (at school): For personalized learning, recommending relevant books, connecting you with other students of the same grade in chats, and guiding our AI assistant to focus on your specific grade level.

We also collect certain information automatically as you interact with our Service, which may include:

  • IP Address: For general location, security, and advertising purposes.
  • Device Identifiers: Such as unique device IDs, for analytics and advertising purposes.

We do not collect any sensitive personal data (e.g., health information, racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, genetic or biometric data).


2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected personal data for the following specific purposes:

  • Account Management: Your Name and Email are used for account creation, login, and managing your user profile.
  • Communication: Your Name and Email are used for communication within the Service's chat features.
  • Personalized Learning: Your Grade is used to provide a personalized learning experience, including:
    • Showing books and learning materials relevant to your grade level.
    • Facilitating connections with other students who have the same grade in chat groups.
    • Directing our AI assistant to tailor its assistance and content to your specific grade.
  • Advertising: We use collected data, including IP address and device identifiers, to serve and measure the effectiveness of advertisements, which may include personalized ads.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling beyond the personalized learning functionalities and advertising purposes described above.


3. How We Share Your Information

We share your personal data with our cloud service provider (Firebase or Supabase) for the sole purpose of secure data storage.

We also share certain information with third-party advertising partners to serve and measure advertisements on our Service. These partners may use the information to deliver ads that are more relevant to your interests.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).


4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data (Name, Email, and Grade, along with automatically collected data) only for as long as your account is active on LT. Once you delete your account, your personal data will be securely deleted from our systems.


5. Your Privacy Rights

As a user of LT, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you be corrected or updated.
  • Right to Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data by deleting your account through your account settings.

Exercising Your Rights:

You can exercise these rights directly through your account settings within the LT platform.

Please note that the use of your grade is essential for accessing and utilizing the core personalized learning features of the LT app, and therefore, you cannot object to or restrict its processing while maintaining access to the Service.


6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We utilize the security features provided by our cloud service providers, Firebase or Supabase, to protect your information. These platforms incorporate robust security measures such as encryption, firewalls, secure servers, and access controls to help prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your personal data.


7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies for essential functionalities like saving your login session through your browser. These are necessary for the proper functioning of the Service and to allow you to remain logged in.

Additionally, we use these technologies for advertising purposes, including serving personalized ads and measuring ad effectiveness.

You can typically manage or disable cookies through your web browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the LT website, particularly your ability to log in and stay logged in. You may also be able to opt-out of personalized advertising through device settings or industry opt-out programs (e.g., NAI, DAA).


8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and through our Telegram channel. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the terms.
